Study Abroad In Greece

Spring 2022 Tippie RISE 0 Comments

During the spring semester of 2022, I had the privilege of studying abroad in Thessaloniki, Greece, where I engaged in an enriching academic and cultural experience. I pursued finance classes that offered me a comprehensive understanding of finance from an international perspective, a valuable asset for my future career. Additionally, I acquired an international sea sailing license through a sea sailing class, which was a completely new experience. My travels across Europe provided an opportunity to immerse myself in various histories and cultures, leaving me with a broader perspective and enriched personal growth. My study abroad experience has taught me essential life skills and has had a profound impact on my professional and personal development. I am deeply grateful for this transformative experience.

Enterprise Risk Management Experiential Project

Fall 2022 RMI Certificate 0 Comments

In my enterprise risk management class, I had the opportunity to work on an experiential project with our client, Guy Carpenter, to review reinsurance limits. The project entailed working collaboratively with my team and the client to analyze and evaluate the risk management strategies employed by Guy Carpenter. Through this process, we gained valuable insights into the intricacies of reinsurance and the challenges that firms face in managing their risks. Throughout the semester, we engaged in constructive discussions and received guidance from our client, which helped us develop a better understanding of the risk management profession. This project enabled me to hone my analytical and critical thinking skills while gaining practical experience in the field of risk management.
